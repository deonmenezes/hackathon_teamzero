Overview

The Spiti Valley Slow Travel experience offers a unique way to explore the serene landscapes of the high-altitude Himalayan desert. This trip is perfect for travelers looking to immerse themselves in local culture, sustainable tourism, and adventure.

Discover ancient monasteries, traditional Spitian homestays, and eco-friendly trekking routes. Enjoy breathtaking mountain views, visit remote villages, and experience the slow-paced lifestyle of Spiti Valley.

Itinerary
  • Day 1: Arrival in Kaza & Homestay Check-in
  • Day 2: Explore Key Monastery & Langza Village
  • Day 3: Trekking & Interaction with Locals
