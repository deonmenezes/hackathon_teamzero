Overview

The Eco-Friendly Sundarbans Wildlife Retreat offers a unique opportunity to explore the largest mangrove forest in the world. This trip is designed for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and eco-conscious travelers.

Experience the rich biodiversity of Sundarbans, home to the majestic Royal Bengal Tiger, saltwater crocodiles, and diverse bird species. Enjoy sustainable accommodations, boat safaris, and interactions with the local community.

  • Day 2: Sunrise Boat Safari & Wildlife Spotting
